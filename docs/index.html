Core Features

Everything you need for acute care at your fingertips

⏱️

CPR Timer

Interactive cardiac arrest timer with automatic 2-min cycles, epinephrine alerts, shock counter & auto-generated report.

📝

ABCDE Assessment

Structured trauma report with automatic severity triage and bilingual clinical narrative generation.

🧮

40+ Calculators

GCS, SOFA, APACHE II, CURB‑65, Wells, CHA₂DS₂‑VASc, HAS‑BLED, HEART, NIHSS & more validated scores.

🫁

Blood Gas Analysis

Acid-base classification, A‑a gradient, P/F ratio, and ARDS Berlin classification.

💉

Vasoactive Drugs

Norepinephrine, epinephrine, dobutamine, dopamine infusion calculators with customizable dilution.

😴

Sedation Protocols

RSI protocols with weight‑based dosing, preoxygenation checklist & procedural sedation.

💧

Metabolic Disorders

Dysnatremia, dyskalemia, dyscalcemia correction using Adrogué‑Madias formula.

💊

Antibiotic Guide

Spectrum, dosing, renal adjustment & major drug interactions for 50+ antibiotics.

📋

55 Protocols

Emergency department clinical pathways and management guidelines.
