The app follows Clean Architecture with four top-level packages under lib/: core (shared infrastructure — theme, l10n strings, utilities like DeferredWidget for lazy loading, reusable animated widgets), domain (business entities and use cases), data (datasources and models such as SedationData, VasoactiveData, UserModel extending User), and presentation (Riverpod providers and screens). Entry point lib/main.dart bootstraps ProviderScope from flutter_riverpod, wires MaterialApp.router with AppTheme light/dark themes, and watches routerProvider/themeProvider/languageProvider. Tests under test/unit cover calculator logic and datasources (antibiotics, blood gas, metabolic, sedation, vasoactive) via scenario-driven assertions; a single widget_test.dart exists at the root.
